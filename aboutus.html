Our organization believes there are solutions to every problem and that most problems can be solved by understanding, cooperation, and

compromise. We believe that smart management and moderation are the keys to long-term success for any industry—be it fishing and waste

management. We strive to be non-political and unbiased and to let science dictate both the problems and the solutions.

We hope that we can educate more people about the marine life through our website. We need to face and understand environmental

problems to be able to solve them,but despair alone doesn’t lead to positive outcomes. It is easy to become overwhelmed

by the vastness of the task of reversing the alarming damage to so many aspects of the natural world.

We need stories about people working together to make a difference to inspire our own small steps.

We are determined to inform readers about threats, consequences and solutions based on scientific facts, not political

prejudice or business interests.Last but not least, we hope we can spread the awareness with lots of activities

and campaigns to preserve our marine life
